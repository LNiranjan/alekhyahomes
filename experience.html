At Alekhya Homes, we are happy to say that we have built our business on honesty, integrity and attention to detail. We believe that the moment someone steps into the gate of an apartment complex or a house, they need to be transported into a space that is at once welcoming, comforting and distinct. The Alekhya Experience is characterized by complete adherence to our design values and principles. We believe in creating homes whose beauty is marked by simplicity, derived from nature and above-all is organic in architecture. Each home we build is characterized by sensitivity to site and structure. Our design values and principles guide us in every project we take up and ensure that the outcome is always delightful to our clients


THE BUYING EXPERIENCE



When you engage with Alekhya Homes, we make sure that every interaction is delightful and enriching. Because we believe that the joy of owning the home of your dreams should start with the planning stage. In us, you will find trusted friends who will advise you and hold your hand through every step of the buying process. Our team will help you identify the home that best fits your needs and budgets. And explain each and every detail of all the processes that follow. From signing up to make a purchase to the handover of the house keys. Being completely transparent in our processes, we will ensure that there is no ambiguity about status and schedules at any point of time. Because we believe that we are building something more important than homes. Relationships.


THE LIVING EXPERIENCE



Aside from the fact that every construction of Alekhya Homes meets the highest standards in building and materials that age gracefully over the years, we do not wash off our hands from a project after it is delivered. Rather, we take an active part in maintaining and evolving the space. Whether it is developing community parks around the site or creating a fertile base for biodiversity, or even performing regular checks on the general upkeep of the property. We feel responsible for ensuring that the homes we build always appreciate in value and continue to delight those who inhabit it. Because we believe that our relationship does not end when you take possession of your home from us. It just begins




TRADITIONAL

We draw tremendous value and learning from traditional Indian architecture. Whether is it detailing nooks and corners within homes such as pergolas and urulis, or the kind of finish we give to the wood and stone we use in construction, the ultimate form of our design is distinctively traditional.




SIMPLE

We believe that beauty and utility need not be overstated and so, our buildings are characterized by clean lines and minimalism lending a fresh and contemporary look to them. A lot of times, our decisions of whether to choose a design or not boils down to whether it is simple enough or not.




NATURAL

Our homes derive their sense of design and colors from nature and are also built in harmony with nature. Irrespective of where you are standing in one of our constructions, your eyes will see a part of nature. Our homes are punctuated with organic living plants that are native to the region.




CUSTOMIZABLE

We understand that each of our clients has a different vision for their dream home. We work very closely with each client to understand their specific requirements and translate them into architectural forms that are both aesthetic and functional. Ultimately, people become extensions of their homes and vice versa.




SUSTAINABLE

Being strict adherents to the principles of sustainable development, we take care to see that we source materials that are locally available. We go the extra mile to construct rainwater harvesting pits at each project we undertake.





DESIGN PRINCIPLES


Design: implement design learning gleaned from the masters of the field - the value of using locally available materials from Laurie Baker, the power of building experiential architecture from Geoffrey Bawa and the importance of blending landscaping with living from Jamie Durie.


Materials: Our homes are constructed with natural and aesthetic materials. In our projects we regularly use stones such as Jaisalmer, Kota, Tandur Blue, Guntur Durgi and naturally formed rocks from river beds. We embellish home interiors with terracotta works, uruli, stone carvings, handmade tiles inlays from Pondicherry and Athangudi, lending natural textures and colors to spaces. In addition to adding distinct aesthetic value, these materials improve living conditions through their function as well.

Value for Money: are builders with a conscience; we know the sacrifices you make to have your own house. In return for the immense trust that you place in us, we ensure that we provide you value for money by providing good designs and good quality material. We make sure that you are proud of the place you live in for a long time.


Simple and Elegant Design: designs are simple, elegant and understated. One look at our houses and you will know that we believe that beauty doesn't need to be overstated. The characteristic features of our houses are simple clean lines and cuts with contemporary design.


Transparency and Ethics: understand that in most cases, a house is a huge investment both emotionally and financially and that your peace of mind is largely dependent on knowing that it is in safe hands. Through regular and transparent communication, we ensure that our clients are assured and at peace about their investments.
