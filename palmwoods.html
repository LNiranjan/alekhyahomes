Alekhya's Palm Woods gifts you the best of both worlds. It is located amidst the pulsing throbbing nerve centre of the most happening city, with easy commute to major IT companies, financial institutions and educational institutions, yet offering a world nestled in nature. Palm Woods faces unending greens of Emaar Golf Club, and is comfortably nuzzled between Gachibowli ORR and Wipro Circle.
